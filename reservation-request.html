Request your reservation

Fill out the form and we'll contact you to schedule your lesson, demonstration, or event. This is a request, we'll confirm dates, locations, and details with you directly.

What happens next?

  1. Submit your request with preferred dates and session type.
  2. We call you to compare schedules and discuss details.
  3. Review and sign safety waivers before your first flight.
  4. Show up ready to fly. We bring boards, helmets, and life jackets.

Reservations are not confirmed until we contact you and all documents are reviewed and signed.

Your safety is our top priority. Life jackets and helmets are required and provided by CharlotteEfoil (or bring your own). All students must be able to swim. Waiver forms will be on site before your first flight.

How rentals work

Minimum 90 minute morning and afternoon sessions, 7 days a week (weather permitting). One person, one board. We cannot share time on a single board.

Every session includes ~15 minutes of safety instruction and getting acquainted with the eFoil at a pre selected public or private launch site, followed by our 4 step learning process.

Scheduling flexibility

  • Standard 90 min morning sessions (8am–10am)
  • Standard 90 min afternoon sessions (5pm–7pm)
  • Flexible times. Tell us your preference
  • Sunrise, sunset & group foiling sessions
  • Single rider to full week reservations
